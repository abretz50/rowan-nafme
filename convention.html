Registration Information

Registration will open at the end of August 2025. Categories include active members, retired members, collegiate members, clinicians, and non-members. All registrations must be completed online.

Early registration: Open now through February 6, 2026.
Late registration: Available after February 6, 2026, for an additional fee. Late registration will also be available on-site.

Membership Requirement: To register as an Active Member, you must have a NAfME/NJMEA membership current through 02/21/26. Registration will remain pending until your membership is renewed if it is not up-to-date.
