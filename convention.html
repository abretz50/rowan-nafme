2026 NJMEA State Conference

Dates: February 19–22, 2026

Location:
Convention Center, Atlantic City, NJ
1 Convention Blvd
Atlantic City, NJ 08401

The 2026 NJMEA State Conference will be held at the Atlantic City Convention Center. The conference hotel is the Sheraton, which has an enclosed walkway that takes you directly to the Convention Center.

What to Expect

Join us for a weekend of more than 200 Professional Development Workshops and Performances ! From Thursday February 19th through Saturday February 22nd our Chapter will be staying in Atlantic city for the 2026 NJMEA State Conference. Student who attend will participate in Professional Devlopments, attend lectures from leaders in our field, witness performances from statewide ensembles, and so much more! The networking, career skills, and insights gained from this conference have consistenly insighted a positive disposition in all our educators who have had the opportunity to attend.

Registration Information

The top point earners in our chapter will receive free conference registration and a free NAfME membership to join us on the 2026 Conference trip. We will stay overnight in the Sheraton. If you are not a top point earner you may still join us on the trip by paying your own registration and membership fees, everyone will need to pay for their hotel rooms($50 a person).

Conference Registration: Open August 2025 through February 6, 2026.
NAfME membership Registration:

Membership Requirement: To register as an Active Member, you must have a NAfME/NJMEA membership current through 02/21/26. Registration will remain pending until your membership is renewed if it is not up-to-date.
