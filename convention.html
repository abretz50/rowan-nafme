Events & Experiences

Convention Center

The venue offers flexible rooms, on-site parking, and a secure, attendee-friendly layout with a direct connection to the Sheraton.

Accommodations

We’ll stay at the Sheraton (attached to the convention center). Amenities include a 24-hour fitness center, gift shops, and a ballroom. Rooms sleep up to four; we’ll group assignments and collect preferences.

Vendor Expo

Explore new resources, technologies, and instrument care—with chances to learn about summer development programs and network with educators and vendors.

Professional Developments

Choose from a vast catalog spanning early childhood through collegiate topics—led by new teachers, veteran educators, and professors across specialties.

Reading Sessions

Participate in score reading sessions (instrumental and vocal), discover emerging repertoire, and take home free copies of the music you read.

Performances

Enjoy scheduled performances by high schools, colleges, and professional ensembles throughout the event—watch the schedule for details.

Overview

What to Expect

Join us for a weekend of more than 200 professional development workshops and performances. Students attend PD sessions, lectures from leaders in our field, statewide ensemble performances, and networking events that build practical career skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the NJMEA conference?

Any Rowan student can attend. If you’re not in the points cohort, you’re still welcome—just plan to cover your own registration and membership fees.

How do carpools and room shares work?

We’ll collect preferences and group students into carpools and hotel rooms (up to four per room) ahead of the conference.

What should I budget?

Top points earners receive free registration and NAfME membership. Everyone splits the hotel cost (target $50/person) and handles meals on their own.

When will the detailed schedule be available?

The official schedule usually posts closer to the event. We’ll link it on this page as soon as it’s live and highlight recommended sessions.
