President
Hi! My name is Min Hwang and I am your Rowan NAfME President. I am currently in my third year at Rowan and I double major in Music Education and Psychology. My primary instrument is the violin but I've marched 4 years of mellophone and am now one of the Rowan University Marching Band drum majors. In my free time I like to play volleyball or go running. Cameron Hyson
Vice President
Cameron Hyson is a tenor ll from Millville New Jersey. He is a Music Education major working to become a great educator. He has been teaching within church ministries his entire life, and is currently involved in the Rowan Concert Choir, The American Choral directors association, and much more. As the NAfME vice president Cameron is always free to talk about questions or concerns regarding events, PD’s, or general meetings.
Jessenia Zavala
Treasurer
Jessenia Zavala is a junior Vocal Music Education major at Rowan University and is excited to be serving as Treasurer for NAfME this year! In this role, she will oversee the club’s finances, help plan fundraisers and bake sales, and work with the e-board to lead engaging, interactive meetings for members. Jessenia also serves as Treasurer for ACDA, is a proud member of Tri Alpha, sings with Concert Choir, and studies voice with Professor Marian Stieber. She has previously performed with Rowan’s Opera Company, appearing as the Sandman in Hansel and Gretel and performing in the Fall Opera Scenes. Jessenia has also performed in several productions at Ocean County College, including All Shook Up, Kiss Me Kate, and Broadway for the Holidays 2 & 3.
Makayli Matias
Secretary
Makayli Matias is a sophomore studying Vocal Music Education. She is thrilled to serve as Secretary for both Rowan’s NAfME and ACDA chapters this year, managing communication and tracking point totals for each organization. Makayli sings with both the Concert Choir and Voces ensemble and is also a member of Rowan’s Opera Company. Most recently, she was seen as the Dew Fairy in Hansel and Gretel. She looks forward to continuing to grow as a musician and leader through her involvement at Rowan.
Emilee Townsley
Social Media Coordinator
Emilee Townsley is a music education major with a concentration in voice. Going into her senior year, she is very thrilled to fulfill her role as social media coordinator for NAfME. In this position, she will be keeping the Rowan NAfME social platforms updated with the latest news on meetings and events, both inside the chapter and beyond. In addition, she will be co-managing the chapter’s website as well as staying in communication with neighboring collegiate chapters. Emilee is also involved in Rowan’s concert choir, Voces ensemble and ACDA chapter.
Adam Bretz

Adam is an Electrical & Computer Engineering Major with a minor in music(Organ Performance). In his role he attends Student Government meetings and represents the club's interests to the school. Aside from Adam's primary role he also maintains the chapter's website.
