Jessenia Zavala
Treasurer
Jessenia Zavala is a junior Vocal Music Education major at Rowan University and is excited to be serving as Treasurer for NAfME this year! In this role, she will oversee the club’s finances, help plan fundraisers and bake sales, and work with the e-board to lead engaging, interactive meetings for members. Jessenia also serves as Treasurer for ACDA, is a proud member of Tri Alpha, sings with Concert Choir, and studies voice with Professor Marian Stieber. She has previously performed with Rowan’s Opera Company, appearing as the Sandman in Hansel and Gretel and performing in the Fall Opera Scenes. Jessenia has also performed in several productions at Ocean County College, including All Shook Up, Kiss Me Kate, and Broadway for the Holidays 2 & 3.
Makayli Matias
Secretary
Makayli Matias is a sophomore studying Vocal Music Education. She is thrilled to serve as Secretary for both Rowan’s NAfME and ACDA chapters this year, managing communication and tracking point totals for each organization. Makayli sings with both the Concert Choir and Voces ensemble and is also a member of Rowan’s Opera Company. Most recently, she was seen as the Dew Fairy in Hansel and Gretel. She looks forward to continuing to grow as a musician and leader through her involvement at Rowan.
