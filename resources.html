Rowan Music Education

Our Curriculum ▾

Rowan's Music Education program is a double major — B.M. in Music Education and B.A. in Education — preparing students to teach K–12. Two tracks:

  • Instrumental Music Education
  • Vocal Music Education

More details on the Rowan Music Education webpage.
